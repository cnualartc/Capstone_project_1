Featured Bands

Green day

An American rock band formed in the East Bay of California in 1987

Grammy Award winner for Record of the Year in 2006, Best Rock Album in 2010; among others.

Incubus

An American rock band formed in Calabasas, California in 1991

Won the Billboard Music Award for Modern rock Track of the Year in 2001.

Evanescence

An American rock band founded in Little Rock, Arkansas in 1995

Grammy Award winner for Best New Artist in 2004, among several other awards.

Metallica

An American heavy metal band formed in Los Angeles, California in 1981.

Grammy Award winner for Best Metal Performance in 1999, 2004, 2009; among many other awards.

Pearl Jam

An American rock band formed in Seattle, Washington in 1990.

Grammy Award winner for Best Hard Rock Performance in 1996, among several other awards and nominations.

Korn

An American nu metal band formed in Bakersfield, California in 1993.

Grammy Award winner for Best Music Video in 2000, Best Metal Performance in 2003, and other awards.

Nickelback

A Canadian rock band formed in Hanna, Alberta in 1995.

Won the Billboard Music Award for Top Duo/Group in 2006 and the Juno Award for Album of the year in 2009; among others.

Red Hot Chili Peppers

An American rock band formed in Los Angeles, California in 1982.

Grammy Award winner for Best Rock Album and Best Rock Performance in 2007; along with many other awards and nominations.
